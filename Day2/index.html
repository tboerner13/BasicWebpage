How to Train Your Dragon

HTTYD is an amazing movie filled with vikings and dragons. At first the vikings believe that they need to destroy the dragons because they are stealing their livestock, but one scrawny viking shows them that dragons are so much more than meets the eye after he regrettingly shoots down one of the most feared dragons in existence.
